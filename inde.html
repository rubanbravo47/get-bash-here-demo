Naruto Shippuden

Naruto: Shippuden is an anime television series mainly adapted from Part II of Masashi Kishimoto's
original Naruto manga series, with exactly 500 episodes.
It is set two and a half years after the original series in the Naruto universe,
following the ninja teenager Naruto Uzumaki and his allies. The series is directed by Hayato Date,
and produced by Pierrot and TV Tokyo. It began broadcasting on February 15, 2007, on TV Tokyo, and concluded on March 23, 2017.
